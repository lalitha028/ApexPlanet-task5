AH-64 Apache

Overview

The AH-64 Apache is the US Army's primary attack helicopter, designed to excel in front-line combat. Equipped with advanced avionics, it delivers powerful firepower in any environment.

Specifications

🛫 Manufacturer: Boeing
⚡ Max Speed: 182 mph
🌍 Range: 300 miles
👨‍✈️ Crew: 2
🔫 Armament: 30mm chain gun, Hellfire missiles, rockets

Mission Steps

Arm weapons → Mission briefing → Takeoff → Attack target → Return to base
