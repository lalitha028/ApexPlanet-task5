C-130 Hercules

Overview

The C-130 Hercules is a military transport aircraft that has served nations globally for over 60 years. It is renowned for its ruggedness and ability to operate in harsh environments, from short runways to unprepared airstrips.

Specifications

⚡ Max Speed: 366 mph
🌍 Range: 2,360 miles
👨‍✈️ Crew: 4-5
📦 Cargo Capacity: 45,000 lbs
