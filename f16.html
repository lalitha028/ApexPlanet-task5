F-16 Fighting Falcon

Overview

The F-16 Fighting Falcon is a compact, multi-role fighter aircraft that has been a key part of air forces worldwide since 1978. Known for its agility and versatility, it remains a top choice for aerial combat and defense missions.

Specifications

🛫 Manufacturer: Lockheed Martin
⚡ Max Speed: 1,500 mph
🌍 Range: 2,620 miles
👨‍✈️ Crew: 1
🔫 Armament: 20mm cannon + air-to-air/air-to-ground missiles

Mission Steps

Pre-flight check → Arm weapons → Taxi to runway → Takeoff → Engage target → Return to base
